About Bubbles & Fur

Welcome to Bubbles & Fur, where cleanliness meets compassion. Founded out of a deep joy for maintaining spotless spaces, Bubbles & Fur is more than just a cleaning service—it's a passion project driven by the belief that cleanliness is next to godliness.

With years of experience in both cleaning and animal care, I bring a unique perspective to every home I touch. I've had the privilege of working with Fortune 500 companies, small businesses in the picturesque foothills of Colorado, and esteemed organizations like the Humane Society. My background as a kennel manager and doggie daycare supervisor has given me a special affinity for ensuring not just clean, but also safe and welcoming environments for both people and pets.

At Bubbles & Fur, compassion, understanding, and faith guide every interaction. I believe that everyone deserves to feel appreciated, and my commitment to using all-natural, biodegradable, pet- and child-safe cleaning products reflects this. From bamboo business cards to organic cotton uniforms and reusable cleaning materials, I take pride in offering a service that’s as gentle on the planet as it is on your home.

Customer service is at the heart of what I do. I strive to ensure that every client feels like the most important person in the world, and my love for cleanliness is just the cherry on top. My number one priority is to take care of your home so you can relax and enjoy your sanctuary.

Respecting your needs, being clear and concise in communication, and delivering timely, efficient service are the cornerstones of my business. I go above and beyond to exceed expectations, ensuring happy clients and building lasting relationships.
